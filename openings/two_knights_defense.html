Two Knights Defense

The Two Knights Defense is an aggressive counter to the Italian Game.

  1. e4 e5
  2. Nf3 Nc6
  3. Bc4 Nf6

4. d4

d4 threatens black's centre while also freeing white's dark squared bishop, allowing for quick development.

The only credible response to d4 for black is exd4.

  1. d4 exd4

This position is familiar to certain lines of the Scotch Gambit.
